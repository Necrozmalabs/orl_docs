#### Why Choose Necrozma Labs?

- **Component Accuracy**: All components are modeled after real-world lab instruments with high simulation fidelity.
- **Flexible Experimentation**: Use one or multiple components in a single experiment with customizable input parameters.
- **Fair Credit System**: All users start with 100 credits. Components range from 3 to 10 credits depending on complexity.
- **Open & Inclusive**: Whether you’re a university researcher, indie scientist, or a curious student — we welcome you.
